# SparkReceipt

AI-powered receipt scanning and expense tracking for freelancers and small businesses.

SparkReceipt eliminates manual bookkeeping by using AI to extract, categorize, and organize financial documents automatically. Snap a photo, upload a file, or forward an email receipt — SparkReceipt handles the rest.

## What SparkReceipt Does

- Receipt scanning — AI extracts every detail from receipts in seconds
- Expense & income tracking — Automatically categorizes and organizes transactions
- Email receipt forwarding — Forward email receipts for automatic scanning and categorization
- Bank statement matching — Upload PDF, Excel, or CSV statements and match transactions to receipts with AI
- Expense reports — Export clean, organized reports in PDF, Excel, CSV, or ZIP
- Multi-language OCR — Process receipts in various languages and character sets
- 150+ currencies — Automatic exchange rate conversion
- Sub-accounts — Manage multiple businesses or personal finances in separate workspaces
- Accountant collaboration — Invite your accountant for free access to organized documents
- Integrations — QuickBooks Online sync, plus webhooks for e.g. Zapier, Make.com, and Pabbly
